Founders

Pablo Solano, Co-Founder

Pablo Solano is an investor in Silicon Valley, having placed upwards of $200M of family office capital into high-profile technology enterprises including Lyft, SpaceX, Airbnb, and Singularity University. As private technology opportunities grow in value, Solano has specialized in technology investment access and diversification: helping large single-family and multi-family investment offices diversify in category-leading companies both in late stage venture capital and high-growth sectors of the economy. He has developed a global network of investors that span from the Americas to Asia and splits his time between his home country Costa Rica, Miami, and San Francisco.

Baran Dilaver, Co-Founder

Baran is an entrepreneur, creator and an accomplished executive. He has launched over a dozen well-known brands worldwide and developed many award-winning products. Baran has served as the COO and CEO at public and private companies, most recently as COO at Namaste Technologies, a leading cannabis company publicly listed at TSE. His last venture, Firefly Vapor, where he performed as the COO and CMO, recently sold to Slang International which is set for IPO in December of 2019. Baran spent his career in leading companies in consumer products where partnered with a wide range of prominent institutions including Starbucks, Diageo, Walmart, and Four Seasons Hotels.

He is a speaker in conferences like Tech Open Air Berlin, CannaTech and European Communications Summit. He completed his undergraduate studies at U.C. Berkeley where he earned athletic and academic scholarships and studied Economics. One of his passions over the past 15 years has been building large-scale art installations at Burning Man.

Elie Riachi, Partner

Elie, based out of London, is an Independent Consultant for European Family Offices & Private investors, having raised a total of $ 200MN for US/European startups in seed or Series A stage in sectors such as e-commerce, patent solutions, cannabis, pharmaceuticals and for real estate Fund, Kronos IG, focused on Spain ...Elie was previously at Credit Suisse (1999-2009) in European Sales trading at the time of the hot tech IPO era under Mr Frank Quatronne.

Ali Dagli, Partner

Today one of the most trusted thought leaders in Silicon Valley, Ali Dagli is a tech entrepreneur and a deal-maker. Currently, he is the co-founder and partner of Snow Factory, startup studio based in New York. Ali has had multiple executive positions at leading startups and previously spent 13 years in tech investment banking. During his investment banking tenure as a Partner at GP Bullhound and a Vice President at GCA Savvian, he spearheaded Consumer Internet franchises and has executed over 60 private placement, M&A, private and strategic advisory mandates.

Ali involves himself in enduring opportunities that identify potential and product-market fit, execute highly strategically, connect synergistic partners, and transform opportunity into a sustainable enterprise.
