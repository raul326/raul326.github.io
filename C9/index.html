Main Adobe VIP Benefits

You can now access Adobe products directly in ACMP, provisioning end customers with Adobe subscription licenses quicker. Increase your company profits by setting up customers on auto renewal subscriptions and reduce your admin time by directly managing new and current customers. Review these main Adobe VIP benefits.

Earn more revenue

Adobe VIP uses auto-renewal subscriptions providing resellers with a source of predictive cash flow. If a customer doesn’t want the license renewal, simply reduce the number of seats in the ACMP before the deadline. Enjoy the use of the purchased licenses until the end date.

EInvoice customers daily

Anticipate your cash flow with daily customer invoicing - and not monthly. Meaning if your customers have 244 days until the end date, they will be invoiced for the 244 days.

A seamless customer experience

No portal changes will happen for end customers. Your customers will continue to access the same admin console meaning they won’t even notice the change.

EOne-click onboarding

It only takes one minute to get started as a new certified Adobe reseller. Say goodbye to the 14-day start-up period and directly access Adobe VIP in the ACMP.

Add licenses quickly

Have your first license up and running in as little as 10-minutes.

Easy Setup For ALSO Resellers

The Adobe VIP is an alternative to the classic VIP licensing program. There’s no requirement to be a certified Adobe partner to access. Purchasing customers cannot see Adobe VIP in your reseller portal but must be handled exclusively in the ACMP.
