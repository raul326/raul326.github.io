Charlotte Greenwood

Charlotte studied fine art in London, where she earned a Bachelor’s degree in Sculpture, from Chelsea College of Art and Design, and National Diploma in foundation studies, from Central St.Martin’s School of Art and Design. Charlotte’s work can be found in private collections throughout Europe and North America.

Alireza Shojaian

Alireza Shojaian is an Iranian artist, born in 1988 in Tehran. He studied at IAU Art and Architecture University in Tehran, and obtained his masters in Fine Arts. He now spends time between Tehran and Beirut.
