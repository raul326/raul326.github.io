Charlotte Greenwood

Artist / Coach

About the Artist

Visual artist Charlotte Greenwood, born in London in the UK, started her career at the age of 8, with magazine publicities, mural designs, and fine art painting, followed by a 20 year long career in cinema, working as a special effects makeup artist, set sculptor and model maker, in Europe and North America. Since 2008 she has been focusing on painting and drawing. In 2012 her work was exhibited in London and New York, and in her first solo show in a public gallery, ​Galerie D​, Montreal. Since then she has been part of group exhibitions in Nova Scotia, Ottawa and Montreal, and had her work selected in the PAS Biennial juried exhibition, where her work won the Founders prize in 2017.

Charlotte studied fine art in London, where she earned a Bachelor's degree in Sculpture, from Chelsea College of Art and Design, and National Diploma in foundation studies, from Central St.Martin’s School of Art and Design. Charlotte’s work can be found in private collections throughout Europe and North America.

Pencil Stories

Pencil Collection: You

Project YOU is a celebration of the differences between us, and a homage to the stories we all have lived, which make us the fascinating characters we are. It goes beyond borders and dives into the heart of the human experience highlighting the extraordinary characters and communities of the world. Project YOU reminds us to appreciate the people around us and to go beyond judgment into gratitude and curiosity, which, instead of dividing nations, will help to unify them.
–Charlotte Greenwood
