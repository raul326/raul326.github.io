Tracing Voices is an online platform which partners with inspiring people to showcase select art and social contributions in the form of short documentaries. Tracing Voices presents these documentaries within their biographical, social and commercial context to enable artists and activists to share their story and the causes close to their heart.

About the Project

Virtual reality is the first step in a grand adventure in the landscape of the imagination.
–Frank Biocca

The VR Dream Machine project has leveraged Sofiapulse's AI chatbot technology to deliver VR experiences to children in the following locations over the course of the past three years:

  • Bekaa, Lebanon - Refugee Camp
  • Wagusu, Kenya
  • Montreal, Canada - Children's Hospital

Based on the notion of play therapy, the project aims to inspire children to dream beyond their borders and to overcome the challenges inherit to their environment. With Google Cardboard (approx, $20) and a phone, organizations and activists everywhere can quickly and easily bring the VR Dream Machine project to life in even the most remote communities.
